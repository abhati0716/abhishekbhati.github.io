Welcome to my personal website.

I am Assistant Professor at the Department of Political Science at the Bowling Green State University. My overarching research agenda lies at the interaction of nonprofit and civil society organizations, political science, and public administration. In particular, I am interested in understanding how nonprofit organizations reduce structural social, economic and political inequalities in society and also inequalities within the nonprofit and civil society sector.

At Bowling Green State University, I teach both core and elective courses in the nonprofit studies minor, Master of Public Administration, and Political Science.

I recieved B.A. in Business Economics from the University of Delhi, M.A. in Social Enterpreneurship from the Tata Institute of Social Sciences, Mumbai, and Ph.D. in Public Administration from the University of Nebraka at Omaha.
