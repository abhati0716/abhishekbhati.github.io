    Main dishes

    • Lauki Kofta - Lauki is Indian name of gourd. I grew bottle gourd in my garden and used them for this dish. Grated gourd with onions and other indian spices prepared as ball and them simmered in curry. See the recipe here.

    • Malai Kofta - Grated home made paneer (Indian cheese) with garden potatoes and other indian spices prepared as ball and them simmered in curry. See the recipe here.

    • Chana Masala - Chana is Indian name of chickpeas. I do not use canned chickpeas. Sauted onions, cummins, ginger, garlic, garden tomotoes, chicpeas with Indian spice. Hint of lemon and cilantro in the end. See the recipe here.

    • Dal Makhani - This probably is my fav dish. Dal is a lentils. This is black lentil. Sauted onions, cummins, ginger, garlic, garden tomotoes, dal with Indian spice. Hint of lemon and cilantro in the end. See the recipe here.

    • Allo Gobhi - Allo is potatoes and Gobhi is cauliflower. Sauted onions, cummins, ginger, garlic, garden tomotoes, peas, allo, gobhi with Indian spice. Hint of lemon and cilantro in the end. See the recipe here.
